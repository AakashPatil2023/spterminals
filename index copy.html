About Us

SP TERMINALS is a leading manufacturer of high-quality electrical terminals, specializing in both primary and secondary terminals for instrument transformers. With over 20 years of industry experience, we have built a strong reputation for precision engineering, durability, and reliability.

Our expertise extends beyond terminals to manufacturing hermetic sealing systems and their essential components, such as PRD Gas Valves, ensuring safety and efficiency in power transmission and distribution systems.

Our Product Range Includes:

  • Brass and MS Inserts – High-performance inserts designed for durability and conductivity.
  • Instrument Transformer Terminals – Used in electrical applications for seamless power connectivity.
  • PRD Gas Valves – Essential components in transformer systems, ensuring pressure regulation and safety.
  • Customized Electrical Components – Tailored solutions to meet the specific needs of different industries.

At SP TERMINALS, we prioritize innovation, quality assurance, and customer satisfaction. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced machinery and testing equipment, allowing us to deliver products that meet national and international industry standards.
